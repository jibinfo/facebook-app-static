How to Delete Your Data

  1. Login to Your Account
    Sign in to your ActiKidz account using your credentials.
  2. Go to Profile Settings
    Navigate to your profile page and find the settings section.
  3. Submit Deletion Request
    Click on "Delete Account" and fill in the required information.
  4. Confirmation
    We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account Information: Username, email, password, profile
  • Social Login Data: Facebook, Google login associations
  • Activity Records: Course enrollments, reviews, browsing history
  • Messages: Communication records with platform and merchants
  • Preferences: Notification settings, language preferences

Facebook Login Data

If you used Facebook login, deleting your account will:

  • Disconnect the association with your Facebook account
  • Delete all data we obtained from Facebook (name, email, profile picture)
  • Revoke our access to your Facebook data
